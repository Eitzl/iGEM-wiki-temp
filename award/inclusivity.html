Inclusivity

How our project design, outreach, and team culture actively work toward equity and inclusion in science and in our work's impact.

Inclusivity in Project Design

The central value proposition of our project — dramatically reducing the cost of Melectin — is itself an inclusivity statement. By designing a production platform that doesn't require expensive chromatography infrastructure, we enable research and (eventually) therapeutic access in settings that currently can't afford high-cost peptide reagents.

[Describe other design choices made with accessibility in mind: choosing duckweed (grows in water, no specialized equipment) over tobacco or Arabidopsis; using open-source tools for modeling; publishing all protocols and parts under open licenses.]
