Modeling & Dry Lab

Computational approaches informing our wet lab work: MD simulations of Melectin membrane insertion, codon optimization, and expression yield prediction.

Overview

Our dry lab work served two roles: (1) informing construct design before entering the lab, and (2) interpreting experimental results. We used molecular dynamics simulation to characterize Melectin's membrane interactions, bioinformatic tools for codon optimization and signal peptide selection, and expression modeling to predict yield.
