Parts

BioBrick parts submitted to the iGEM Registry for the 2026 competition, including new parts and improvements to existing parts.

New Parts

The following parts were designed and characterized by the UCSC iGEM 2026 team:

Part Number Name Type Length (bp) Description Characterization
BBa_KUCSC001 Melectin-Lm Coding 54 Melectin (18 aa) codon-optimized for Lemna minor expression. Includes C-terminal amidation signal sequence. Characterized
BBa_KUCSC002 Oleosin-At18.5 Coding 501 Arabidopsis thaliana 18.5 kDa oleosin (At3g01570). Oil-body targeting and scaffold domain for fusion strategy. Characterized
BBa_KUCSC003 TEV-linker DNA 21 TEV protease recognition sequence ENLYFQS with flanking flexible Gly-Ser linker residues. Partially characterized
BBa_KUCSC004 OleosinPromoter-Lm Regulatory ~800 Native Lemna minor oleosin promoter fragment driving expression in frond tissue. [Characterization in progress] In progress
BBa_KUCSC005 OleoMelec-TEV (composite) Composite 576 Full oleosin-TEV-Melectin fusion cassette. BBa_KUCSC002 + BBa_KUCSC003 + BBa_KUCSC001. Characterized
