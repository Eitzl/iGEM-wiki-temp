Overview of experimental modules: cloning, duckweed transformation, oil-body purification, and antimicrobial assays.

Module 1: Construct Assembly & Cloning

[Describe cloning strategy: Gibson Assembly / Golden Gate assembly of oleosin-TEV-Melectin fusion. PCR conditions, restriction digests, ligation, and E. coli DH5α colony screening. Sanger sequencing confirmation.]

Key experiments

  • Overlap extension PCR to join oleosin-linker-Melectin fragments
  • Gibson Assembly into pCAMBIA-based binary vector
  • Colony PCR screening with construct-specific primers
  • Sanger sequencing of 5 positive clones
  • Restriction digest confirmation of insert size

[Describe triparental mating or direct electroporation of binary vector into Agrobacterium EHA105. Antibiotic selection scheme (rifampicin + kanamycin / hygromycin). Verification of A. tumefaciens harboring construct by colony PCR.]

Module 4: Oil-Body Isolation & TEV Cleavage

[Describe tissue homogenization conditions (buffer composition, pH, protease inhibitors). Centrifugation protocol for oil-body flotation. Washing steps. TEV protease incubation conditions. Second centrifugation to separate cleaved peptide.]

Module 5: Antimicrobial Activity Assays
